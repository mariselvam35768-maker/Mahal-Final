Tables in database:
- admin
- bookings
- contact
- explore
- gallery
- halls
- payments
- room_categories
- rooms
- settings
- slots
- users

Columns in 'users' table:
- id (int(11))
- name (varchar(255))
- email (varchar(255))
- phone (varchar(20))
- password (varchar(255))
- role (enum('admin','user'))
- created_at (timestamp)

Columns in 'admin' table:
- id (int(10))
- email (varchar(50))
- password (varchar(100))
- name (varchar(100))
- role (varchar(50))
